The Best Movies According to Akshay

My top 3 movies of all time.

Zindagi Na Milegi Dobara

AThree friends decide to turn their fantasy vacation into reality after one of their friends gets engaged.

Rockstar

A young musician seeks heartbreak from a popular girl, to further his career and develop a deeper experience of life like his musical heroes.

Kabhi Khushi Kabhi Gham

After marrying a poor woman, rich Rahul is disowned by his father and moves to London to build a new life. Years later, his now-grown younger brother Rohan embarks on a mission to bring Rahul back home and reunite the family.
